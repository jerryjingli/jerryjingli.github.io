Deep Learning for Named Entity Recognition: A Comprehensive Survey

By Li Jing

A survey on deep learning in named entity recognition.

Named entity recognition (NER) is a subtask of information extraction that seeks to locate and classify named entities in text into pre-defined categories (e.g., organization, location and person). Recently, deep learning has been broadly applied to solve NER problem with great success. Thus, I write up this survey to summarize recent work.
