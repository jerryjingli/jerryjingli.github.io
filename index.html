Biography

Mr. Jing LI is currently a PhD candidate in Computer Science, at the School of Computer Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Xing Zhenchang. He received his B.E. and M.E. in Electrical Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), China, in 2010 and 2013, respectively.

Research Interests
  • Data mining and information retrieval in software engineering
  • Pattern mining on sequential data
  • Behavioral biometrics, empirical software engineering
  • Compressed sensing, signal processing
