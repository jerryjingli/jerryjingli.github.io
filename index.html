Biography

Mr. Jing LI is currently a PhD candidate in Computer Science, at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Sun Aixin and Prof. Xing Zhenchang. He received his B.E. and M.E. in Electrical Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), China, in 2010 and 2013, respectively.
